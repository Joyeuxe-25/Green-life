Why Your Gift Matters

Every Contribution Makes a Real Difference

The support of individuals like you makes it possible for Green for Life Rwanda to continue its work engaging small farmers in planting trees, protecting critical forests and watersheds, and empowering communities.

Your support helps to conserve soil and ensure food security, to sustain rural community livelihoods, and to build resilience to climate change. It also enables us to develop tools to reach out and inspire people in Rwanda and around the world to take environmental action.

$5 Plants a tree and supports a farming family
$20 Supports a saving group community meeting
$50 Funds a full youth environmental club session
Any amount Contributes to agroforestry and conservation work

Your Impact

What Your Donation Supports

Agroforestry & Tree Planting

Funding tree nurseries, agroforestry training, and reforestation that restores land and provides income for farming families.

Community Saving Groups

Supporting 19 saving groups with 353 members in Nyanza Sector, Gisagara District to strengthen agricultural production.

Youth Environmental Clubs

Empowering students and young people through environmental clubs, discussions, and hands-on conservation activities.

Farmer Training

Compost making, natural pesticides, and sustainable farming workshops that reduce costs and improve food security.
