Empowering Communities to Protect the Environment and Improve Livelihoods

Green for Life Rwanda is an environmental organization working with communities, including small household farmers, to promote environmental conservation, agroforestry, and sustainable livelihoods.

Who We Are

Dedicated to Environmental Conservation and Community Empowerment

Green for Life Rwanda works hand-in-hand with local communities to restore ecosystems, promote sustainable farming, and build resilience to climate change. Our approach integrates environmental education, agroforestry, and livelihood support to create lasting impact.
