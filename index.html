Who We Are

Community-Based Environmental Action

Green for Life Rwanda (GLR) is an environmental organization that empowers communities, particularly small household farmers, to conserve the environment and improve livelihoods. Our work connects environmental conservation with practical community action.

"Agroforestry is a bridge between sustainable agriculture and environmental conservation."

— World Agroforestry Center (ICRAF)

Our Areas of Work

Agroforestry

We promote agroforestry practices that support agricultural production, food security, soil health, water and air quality, and wildlife habitat for small household farmers.
