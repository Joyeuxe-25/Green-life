Why It Matters

Your Participation Matters

Green for Life Rwanda's work is driven by community involvement. Whether you volunteer, partner with us, or participate in our activities, you help advance environmental conservation, support smallholder farmers, and build a sustainable future for all.

Every action — planting a tree, joining a training, or supporting a saving group — contributes to a cycle of lasting environmental and community change.

Support Our Work

Donate to Green for Life Rwanda

For as little as $5, you can give the gift of environmental conservation. Your contribution helps conserve soil, ensure food security, sustain rural livelihoods, and build community resilience to climate change.

Donations are received through our Bank of Kigali account. Please contact us for full payment details and to confirm your donation.

$5 Plants a tree and supports a farming family
$20 Supports a saving group meeting
$50 Funds a youth environmental club session
