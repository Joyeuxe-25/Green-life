Project Highlights

Tree Nursery

Community Tree Nursery Project

In partnership with local farmers and groups, we support the establishment of small‑scale tree nurseries. These nurseries produce seedlings for reforestation, agroforestry, and fruit production, creating local jobs and improving food security.

  • Native tree species and fruit trees grown
  • Training in nursery management
  • Seedlings distributed to community members

Additional verified details can be added later.

Compost Training

Compost Making Training

Through hands‑on workshops, farmers learn to produce compost from agricultural waste, animal manure, and household scraps. This reduces dependence on chemical fertilizers and improves soil fertility.

  • Practical demonstrations and follow‑up
  • Low‑cost, locally available materials
  • Women and youth actively participate

Expanded information can be included with organization input.

Pesticides Making

Natural Pesticides Production

Participants learn to prepare botanical pesticides using plants like neem, garlic, and chili. These natural alternatives are safer for farmers, consumers, and the environment.

  • Identification of pesticidal plants
  • Preparation techniques
  • Application methods and safety
