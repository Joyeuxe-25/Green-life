April 22, 2023

Nyanza Environmental Clubs Members Trained in Sustainable Agroforestry Practices

On Thursday 21st April 2023, 45 environmental club members from Nyanza Sector, Gisagara District were trained in sustainable agroforestry practices. The training covered agroforestry definitions and key concepts, agroforestry in food security, and the link between poverty and environmental degradation.
