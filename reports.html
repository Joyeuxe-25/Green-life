Our Commitment

Transparency in Everything We Do

Green for Life Rwanda believes that transparency and accountability are the foundation of effective environmental and community work. We are committed to sharing our progress, challenges, and financial information openly with our communities, partners, and supporters.

Our reports will document the real impact of our programs — from saving groups and agroforestry training to youth environmental clubs — ensuring that everyone who supports our work can see exactly how their contributions are used.

Open Reporting Full documentation of activities, outcomes, and finances
Donor Accountability Clear reporting to all supporters and partners
Community Trust Building confidence through honest communication
