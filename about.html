Who We Are

Green for Life Rwanda is an environmental organization dedicated to working hand-in-hand with local communities, especially smallholder farmers, to restore ecosystems, promote sustainable farming, and build resilience to climate change. Our approach integrates environmental education, agroforestry, and livelihood support to create lasting, positive change.

We believe that environmental conservation and community well-being go hand in hand. By empowering people with knowledge and resources, we help them become stewards of their natural environment while improving their own quality of life.
